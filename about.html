About Us

AI Alignment Group at UMD is an organization that seeks to promote and contribute to the alignment of AI systems and the usage thereof with the best interests of humanity: one of the most important, urgent, and interesting challenges of our time. Join us as we learn about and conduct research in topics as varied as catastrophic risks from AI, AI deception, and AI governance.
